Accessibility Statement for Purrfect Paws Cat Hotel

This is an accessibility statement from Purrfect Paws Cat Hotel.

Measures to support accessibility

Purrfect Paws Cat Hotel takes the following measures to ensure accessibility of Purrfect Paws Cat Hotel:

  • Include accessibility as part of our mission statement.
  • Provide continual accessibility training for our staff.
  • Include accessibility throughout our internal policies.

Conformance status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA. Purrfect Paws Cat Hotel is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 level AA. Partially conformant means that some parts of the content do not fully conform to the accessibility standard.

Limitations and alternatives

Despite our best efforts to ensure accessibility of Purrfect Paws Cat Hotel, there may be some limitations. Below is a description of known limitations. Please contact us if you observe an issue not listed below.

Known limitations for Purrfect Paws Cat Hotel:
